Reception Services

Along with our regular Inn and Suites vacation plans, we also offer robust Wedding Reception service packages at extremely competitive prices. All food is provided for, and there are many themes to choose from.

If you reserve a room and stay with us, we offer a 50% discount on all reception fees. It is our goal to ensure that all who stay with us have as little to worry about as possible on their biggest day in eternity.
