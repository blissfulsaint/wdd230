Site Name: McKinney Chamber of Commerce

The purpose of this website will be to further development of the City of McKinney and its citizens by helping business owners, investors, employees, and other such individuals meet with like-minded people through the city's Chamber of Commerce. This website will educate residents on the history of the City of McKinney, be a directory for individuals to find businesses they may be interested in, and allow visitors of the site to become members of the Chamber of Commerce through a tiered membership plan via application. It will also be a source through which to contact the Chamber of Commerce and its board of directors.
