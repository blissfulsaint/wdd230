Target Audience

The target audience for this site will primarily be business owners and investors who are looking for a place to grow their network and get connected with like-minded individuals. These will be people who have fairly busy and fast-paced lives. They will also have families that they help provide for.

Scenarios:

  • Where is the Chamber of Commerce located?
  • What networking events are available in the area?
  • How can I join the Chamber of Commerce?
  • Are there other business owners who can help me know what to do in this situation?
  • Where can I find investors who are willing to invest in my startup?
